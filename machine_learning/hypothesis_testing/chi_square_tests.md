### Proposition 3

$$
\begin{eqnarray}
    \sum_{i=1}^{k}
        \frac{
            (O_{i} - E_{i})^{2}
        }{
            E_{i}
        }
    & = &
        \sum_{i=1}^{k}
            \frac{
                (X_{i} - np_{i})^{2}
            }{
                n p_{i}
            }
\end{eqnarray}
$$
