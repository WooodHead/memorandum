* CPM
    * Cost Per Mille
    * CPM = cost / 1000 impression
* CPA
    * Cost Per Action
    * CPA = cost / action
* CPC
    * Cost Per Click
    * CPC = cost / clicks
* CPS
    * Cost Per Sale
    * CPS = Cost / Sales
* eCPM
    * effective Cost Per Mille
    * eCPM = costs * 1000 / total impression
* CTR
    * Click Through Rate
    * CTR = clicks / impressions

* CVR
    * ConVersion Rate
