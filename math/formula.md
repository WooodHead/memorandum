# formula


## derivative

$$
    \frac{d}{d x} (f(x) g(x) h(x))
        = f'(x) g(x) h(x) 
        + f(x) g'(x) h(x)
        + f(x) g(x) h'(x)
$$

$$
\begin{eqnarray*}
    \begin{array}{rclcccc}
       \frac{d^{2}}{d x^{2}} (f(x) g(x) h(x)) 
        & = &  & f''(x) g(x) h(x) & + & f'(x) g'(x) h(x) & + & f'(x) g(x) h'(x) 
        \\
        & & + & f'(x) g'(x) h(x) & + & f(x) g''(x) h(x) & + & f(x) g'(x) h'(x)
        \\
        & & + & f'(x) g(x) h'(x) & + & f(x) g'(x) h'(x) & + & f(x) g(x) h''(x)
        \\
        & = & & f''(x) g(x) h(x) & + & f(x) g''(x) h(x) & + & f(x) g(x) h''(x) 
        \\
        & & + & 2f'(x) g'(x) h(x) & + & 2f'(x) g(x) h'(x) & + & 2f(x) g'(x) h'(x) 
    \end{array}
\end{eqnarray*}
$$
