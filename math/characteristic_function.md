## Characteristic Function
* $X: \Omega \rightarrow \mathbb{R}$
    * r.v.

Characteristic function of $X$ is defined by

$$
\begin{eqnarray}
    \phi_{X}(t)
    & := &
        \mathrm{E}
        \left[
            e^{-i t X}
        \right]
    .
\end{eqnarray}
$$
