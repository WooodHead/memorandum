## 3.1 Geometry of Convex Sets

### Definition 3.1.6 linearly bounded
* $X$
    * vector sp
* $S \subseteq X$
* $x_{0} \in S$,

$S$ is linearly bounded w.r.t. a point $x_{0}$ if

$$
    \forall y \in S
    \
    \{
        \alpha \in \mathbb{R}_{\ge 0}
        \mid
        (1 - \alpha) x_{0}
        +
        \alpha y
        \in S
    \}
$$

is bounded.
